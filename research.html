Geospatial Dataset for Lahore’s Urban Green Space

Urban Green Space Initiative · CITY, LUMS · Supervised by Dr. Zubair Khalid

Constructed a labelled geospatial dataset for Lahore’s green spaces by fusing Google Maps vegetation masks, OpenStreetMap land-use polygons, and ESA WorldCover layers into a reproducible mapping workflow.

Geographically Generalisable Urban Green Space Segmentation

Urban Green Space Initiative · CITY, LUMS · Supervised by Dr. Zubair Khalid

Explored geographically generalisable approaches for urban green-space segmentation using VHR imagery. YOLOv11n-seg was fine-tuned on curated samples from Islamabad and assessed for transferability across two contrasting regions: Swat and Karachi.

Cartesian: Variance and Gaussian-Based Attention for Urban Green Space Detection Using GANs

Urban Green Space Initiative · CITY, LUMS · Supervised by Dr. Zubair Khalid

Designed variance- and Gaussian-based attention operators to replace average pooling within the GAN discriminator–generator pipeline, yielding improved pixelwise segmentation on Beijing 1 m data and demonstrating cross-domain generalisation on Pakistan’s Sentinel-2A imagery.

GANs Attention mechanisms Urban green space

Developed a real-time remote forest monitoring system combining a distributed PTZ camera network with edge-based fire–smoke detection to provide early alerts in vulnerable regions.
