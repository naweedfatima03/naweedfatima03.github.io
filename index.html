Fatima Naweed

Computer Vision Engineer · Perception for Smart Cities

I am a Computer Vision Engineer at Obvio, Inc., where I work on automated traffic enforcement (ATE) systems designed for real-time traffic monitoring and compliance. I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics from the Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) in 2023. I worked as a Research Assistant with the Computer Vision and Graphics Lab (CVGL) in 2023 and the Centre for Urban Informatics, Technology and Policy (CITY) in 2024.

I’m passionate about using computer vision to help build smarter, safer, and more resilient cities. My interests revolve around anything related to cameras, particularly vision systems inspired by human perception. My work spans real-time urban monitoring, mobility analysis, and crisis mitigation, drawing on object localization and segmentation, 3D scene reconstruction and understanding, multi-sensor perception, and spatiotemporal analysis to build context-aware perception systems for complex urban environments.
