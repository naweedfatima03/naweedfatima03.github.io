Girls Make Games Ambassador

As a Girls Make Games Ambassador, I delivered workshops and hands-on sessions introducing young girls to programming, visual computing, and game development. These workshops focused on creativity, technical confidence, and building early exposure to STEM. They included guided sessions on game design, basic animation workflows, and introductory programming in Unity and Unreal Engine.
