Low-Cost Early Warning System for Forest Fires
RUNNER-UP Impactful Research Team of the Year Award

Recognised at the Triple E Awards 2024 (Asia–Pacific) for impactful research in sustainable environmental monitoring, this project introduced a scalable, camera-based early warning system for forest fires. Deployed in Mansehra, Pakistan, the system used a network of PTZ cameras with real-time fire–smoke detection and automated alerting to enable rapid, low-cost monitoring across remote and high-risk forest regions.
